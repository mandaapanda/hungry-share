GO Types at a Glance
Recurring GO
Auto-generated
  • Created by the schedule engine
  • Fires when a Schedule Rule matches the date
  • Linked to a Schedule via scheduleId
  • Visible to all enrolled users at the location
  • One per location per date
  • Subsidy & UGOM inherited from location
Adhoc GO
CSM Manual
  • Created manually by a CSM for a one-off need
  • Not linked to any Schedule
  • Scoped to a selected subset of enrolled users
  • Multiple can exist on the same date
  • CSM can override subsidy amount & UGOM
  • Cutoff capped at 5 PM the day before
Substitute GO
CSM Manual · Planned
  • Replaces one specific recurring GO instance
  • Linked via replacesRecurringGoId
  • The recurring GO for that date is cancelled
  • Scoped to a subset (can differ from full enrolled set)
  • One substitute per replaced recurring GO per date
  • Subsidy & UGOM rules TBD
How GOs Coexist on a Single Date
Scenario A — Normal week (recurring + adhoc)
Wednesday, May 6 2026
📍 HQ Office
Recurring Weekly Wednesday Lunch All enrolled
Adhoc Executive Team Lunch 8 users
Adhoc New Hire Welcome 5 users
Scenario B — Holiday week (substitute replaces recurring)
Wednesday, May 27 2026
📍 HQ Office
Recurring Weekly Wednesday Lunch Substituted out
Substitute Memorial Day Cookout All enrolled
Adhoc Leadership Off-site 6 users
Key rule: Adhoc GOs are always independent — separate menus, orders, subsidy, and billing. They coexist with recurring and substitute GOs without conflict. A substitute GO only cancels the recurring instance it explicitly replaces; it has no effect on any adhoc GOs on the same date.
